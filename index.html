Research Overview

Our research focuses on the magnetic, dielectric, and structural properties of functional materials, particularly transition metal and rare-earth oxides. The investigation involves studying multiferroic behavior, magneto- and electrocaloric effects, and charge transport mechanisms in these materials.

Using neutron diffraction, synchrotron X-ray techniques, and dielectric spectroscopy, structural distortions and electronic states are analyzed to understand their influence on physical properties. Additionally, X-ray absorption fine structure (XAFS) spectroscopy is utilized to examine local and electronic structures.

Experimental techniques are combined with advanced data analysis tools such as FullProf, JANA, and Demeter to gain insights into the structure-property relationships in condensed matter systems. This work aims to contribute to the development of next-generation functional materials.
